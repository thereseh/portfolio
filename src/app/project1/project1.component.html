Clark CSM

A promotional website for Clark CSM, a graphic design company located in Rochester, NY.

At that time, a friend of mine was an intern for Clark, and she asked me to code a promotional website idea that she had. My friend designed the assets and I did my best to make her ideas a reality considering the short time frame.

I used canvas in order to create interactive elements on the page, such as the hover animations and the moving sprites. A script is used to make the spritesheets come alive, it was the best option as the animations required different speeds in order to look good.

I was also requested to set up a custom design submit form to send data to a google spritesheet. I didn't know much about Ajax back then, but managed to set up a Ajax post request to a google sheet.

I didn't know a lot about web development when I was asked to develop this site. It was a scary request but I decided to give it a shot. It's not one of my best work in terms of code, but it was my greatest learning experience. I had to tackle a lot of challenges and I made it work. I'm no longer intemidated to jump into somehing new, I know that I can handle it.
