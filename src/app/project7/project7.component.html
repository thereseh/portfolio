Asteroids

A basic 2D Asteroid inspired game with my own take on the design.

The bee is your vehicle, and the flowers are the asteroids. Move around the screen using the arrow keys, and shoot with the space bar.

When a flower is hit, it will break up into a random number of smaller pieces. You have 3 lives, and will be "cloaked" for a few seconds after taken damage, which allows you to gain control of the situation.
